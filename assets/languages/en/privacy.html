I. Information Collection and Utilization

1. To log in to this application, you must use your student ID and password. We will collect your name, student ID, college, major, and class through the school's academic management system. If you refuse to provide this information, you will not be able to use our services.

2. When you use the calendar feature, we may request the system to grant access to read, create, modify, and delete calendar entries with your explicit consent, so you can receive timely reminders. If you refuse to provide this information, you will not be able to use the calendar reminder and related features in the app, but this does not affect your use of other features in the app.

3. To write, download, save, modify, or delete installation packages and log files, we request access to your phone storage. If you deny this permission, we cannot perform these actions as requested, but this does not affect your use of other features in this app.

4. When using the class schedule feature, we need access to your Android phone's storage to cache schedule data, enabling offline viewing and proper functioning of the Android desktop plugin.
